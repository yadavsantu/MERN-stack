The Best Movies According to IMDB in 2025

Top 3 movies of 2025

1. A Minecraft Movie

A Minecraft Movie is a 2025 animated film based on the popular video game franchise. The film follows a group of players who are transported into the Minecraft world and must work together to defeat an evil villain threatening their blocky universe.

2.Captain America: Brave New World

Captain America: Brave New World is a 2025 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Captain America. The film follows Sam Wilson, the new Captain America, as he faces a new threat to the world and must prove himself as a worthy successor to Steve Rogers.

3.Mufasa: The Lion King

Mufasa: The Lion King is a 2025 animated film that serves as a prequel to the original Lion King. The film explores the backstory of Mufasa, the beloved lion king, and his rise to power in the Pride Lands.
